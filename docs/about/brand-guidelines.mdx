## Logo

<img src="/brand/logos/pictogrammers-full.svg" alt="Pictogrammers Logo" style="max-width:600px;" />

The Pictogrammers logo is the central visual identity for the Pictogrammers Icon Group. The monogram was created by [Austin Andrews](https://fosstodon.org/@templarian) in 2020. The following year, the wordmark was added by [Michael Irigoyen](https://hachyderm.io/@mririgoyen), completing the logo.

## Wordmark

The Pictogrammers wordmark may exist without the monogram. The typeface used to set the logo wordmark is [Manrope](https://manropefont.com/). The wordmark is optically kerned and its uppercase "P" is customized to mirror the monogram.

## Guidance

Please maintain consistency of our brand by not misusing the Pictogrammers logo, monogram, wordmark, or any of our various icon library logos. If you have a question about whether your use of our logo is accepted, please [get in touch](https://fosstodon.org/@pictogrammers).
